Welcome to Hoteliers!

This is a website that caters to hoteliers worldwide and is still under development.
Here, our users can easily list their hotels and manage them without wasting any more paper.
If you want to manage your reservations more easily as a hotelier, this is the place for you.
At the moment, you can only list your hotels and update their details.


The upcoming updates will bring amazing features to this website. Such features will be:

  • A calendar table that includes all rooms in your hotel will enable you to quickly manage it. You will be able to conveniently colour sections of the calendar table, so you would know which room is reserved, occupied, ready for cleaning and many more.

  • Friends section with a chat system that will allow you and your fellow hoteliers' colleagues to share experiences and help each other with the reservations. Having friends will allow you to enter each other's profiles and check your hotels and photos added to them.
